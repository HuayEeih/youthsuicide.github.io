Youth Suicide

Organization


With the pandemic happening in the whole world currently, it’s understandable that many people are not in the best state of mind. However, don’t be afraid to speak out and seek help. Here are some counselling and mental health services which may be one of the solutions for you to voice out your problems and feel better during this tough period.

MentCouch Psychology Center

  • About: A new mental health clinic in KL managed by certified clinical psychologists, trained therapists, registered counsellors.
  • Services: Adult and children counselling, couple therapy, expressive art therapy, mindfulness training, mental health assessment
  • Address: Suite 1-02, 1st Floor, Menara Atlan, 161 B, Jalan Ampang, 50450 Kuala Lumpur
  • Opening hours: Mon – Fri 9AM-5.30PM | Sat 9AM-1.30PM (Closed on Sundays)
  • Telephone: 03-2712 9372

Befrienders

  • About: An NGO that provide emotional support to people who are distressed, distraught, lonely, or troubled with suicidal thoughts. It is also one of the main suicide hotlines in Malaysia. Conversations with the trained volunteers will be strictly confidential and identity can be anonymous.
  • Services: Counselling, communication
  • Address: 95 Jalan Templer, 46000 Petaling Jaya, Selangor
  • Telephone: 03-7627 2929

Malaysian Mental Health Association

  • About: A non-profit voluntary organisation managed by a committee of professionals.
  • Services: Psychological support, psychiatric rehabilitation programmes, caregiver support service
  • Address: TTDI Plaza, Block A Unit 2-8, Jalan Wan Kadir 3, Taman Tun Dr Ismail, 60000 Kuala Lumpur
  • Opening hours: Mon – Sat 9AM-5PM (Closed on Sundays)
  • Telephone: 03-2780 6803

HumanKind Community Counselling

  • About: A social enterprise that organises workshops and initiatives for vulnerable and at-risk communities, managed by a team of counsellors.
  • Services: Counselling
  • Address: Block B, Level 2, MAHSA Avenue, Jalan Universiti, Off Jalan Ilmu, 59100 Kuala Lumpur
  • Opening hours: Tue – Sun 10AM-7PM (Closed on Mondays)
  • Telephone: 012-300 5453

Soul Mechanics

  • About: A private mental health establishment
  • Services: Individual, couple, and group counselling, art therapy, psychiatry and child psychology, career development programme which provides career assessment for people who feel lost in their professional lives.
  • Address: Giza 03-09, No 12A-3, Jalan PJU 5/15, Kota Damansara, 47810 Petaling Jaya, Selangor
  • Opening hours: 9AM-10PM, Daily
  • Telephone: 010-252 1734 (Gia) | 019-395 9211 (Devi)

Centre for Psychological and Counselling Services(CPCS) by HELP University

  • About: A CPCS managed by qualified professionals.
  • Services: Psychotherapy and psychological assessment, equipped to diagnose adolescents and children who are suffering from neurodevelopmental disorders, provide treatments and therapies include family therapy, expressive art therapy, and anger management.
  • Address: Level 10, Wisma HELP, Jalan Dungun, Medan Damansara, 50490 Kuala Lumpur
  • Opening hours: Mon – Fri 9AM-5.30PM | Sat 9AM-1PM (Closed on Sundays)
  • Telephone: 03-2096 1212

The Mind Faculty

  • About: A private mental health clinic managed by certified specialist, psychiatrists and psychologists
  • Services: Psychiatric, psychological, counselling, therapy
  • Address: Suite 11-G & 11-1, Jalan Solaris 4, Solaris Mont’ Kiara, 50480 Kuala Lumpur
  • Opening hours: Mon – Fri 9AM-9PM | Sat 8AM-7PM (Closed on Sundays)
  • Telephone: 03-2780 6803

Cara Cara

  • About: Managed by a team of certified and registered young therapists, suitable for youths who prefer to talk to somebody of their own age.
  • Services: Couples and family therapy, hypnotheraphy
  • Address: WORQ, Lot 1.02, Level 1, Glo Damansara, 699, Jalan Damansara, Taman Tun Dr Ismail, 60000 Kuala Lumpur
  • Opening hours: 10AM-9PM, Daily
